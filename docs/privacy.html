Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 13, 2026

Cluck ("the App") is developed by Steve Avon ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App operates entirely on your device.

Local Storage

All of your data — including routines, habits, streaks, and settings — is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Notifications

The App may send local notifications (routine reminders and accountability messages). These are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external server. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Audio

The App uses audio playback for wake-up alarms. Audio is played locally and no audio data is collected or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
